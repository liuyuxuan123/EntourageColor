## Development setup
Put _EntourageColor.swift_ into your project to get all extended colors.
Put _EntourageColorUtils.swift_ into your project to get all extended utility functions.
Put _EntourageGradientColor.swift_ into your project to get all gradient colors.
Put _EntourageRandomColor.swift_ into your project to get all random colors.
Put _EntouragePatternColor.swift_ into your project to get all pattern colors.
Put _PokemonCOlor.swift_ into your project to get color scheme of pokemon.
